Privacy Policy — GhostPass

GhostPass stores credentials locally on your device, encrypted with AES-GCM. No passwords or raw biometric data (keystroke timings) are sent to our servers.

  • Data at rest: encrypted per site (HKDF → AES-GCM).
  • Behavioral signals: processed on-device; not uploaded.
  • Optional Pro licensing: license key stored locally.
  • We do not sell or share personal data.
